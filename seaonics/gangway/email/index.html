Product launch

SEAONICS launches a new all-electric gangway for the offshore wind market

SEAONICS accelerates the trend towards electrification and autonomous operations in the renewable offshore wind energy market with the launch of a brand new, all-electric and autonomous gangway that makes all cargo and personnel handling greener and safer.

 

As a specialist in advanced handling systems, with a strong background in deck logistics solutions, SEAONICS has developed a Service Operation Vessel (SOV) concept for efficient cargo and personnel handling.

 

The latest addition to the SOV concept is called the SEAONICS ECMC (Electric Controlled Motion Compensated) Gangway, which includes many significant benefits for the customers, for example:

 

  • The all-electric drive system minimizes risk for oil spill
  • The autonomous landing mode system ensures safer and faster operations
  • Accurate motion control with responsive electric actuators
  • Low power consumption in idling mode

 

The new gangway design is based on SEAONICS’ philosophy of electrifying all handling operations at sea, combined with our extensive experience in control system delivery to the same market.
